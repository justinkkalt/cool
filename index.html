Introduction

 

Welcome to Carroll ISD, where everyone's a Dragon!

Carroll ISD is a K-12 public school system located in the heart of the Dallas-Fort Worth Metroplex. The 21-square-mile district consists of 11 schools serving more than 8,400 students and 1,100 employees. As one of the top school districts in Texas, CISD recently received "A" ratings by the Texas Education Agency at all 11 campuses, and also earned the prestigious Lonestar Cup for the 8th time, winning the most points in 6A history! We invite you to check out our Dragon Scorecard for more details.

        CISD REAL ESTATE OFFERINGS

        The Board has discussed real property during its meetings throughout this fiscal year. The primary reasons for these discussions have been to monetize underutilized properties and reduce District costs.  In August 2023, the Board adopted amended Public-Private Partnership Guidelines in accordance with Texas Government Code Chapter 2267. A public-private partnership is a contract between a governmental entity (such as Carroll ISD) and a private entity for delivering services or facilities in a cost-effective manner. Such a partnership can be more efficient than traditional sources of procurement and public financing. These partnerships offer the opportunity for the District to establish revenue sources that could provide long term benefits to the District. The District is seeking proposals for public-private partnerships as well as bids to purchase or exchange these underutilized properties. The link to the solicitation package with additional details, including how to submit a proposal or bid, MAY BE FOUND HERE. Please register to access bid documents.
